## PFAS statewide monitoring dataset
The [PFAS Statewide Sampling Dataset](https://dataverse.harvard.edu/dataset.xhtml?persistentId=doi:10.7910/DVN/8LPLCF) is a compilation of sampling campaigns of community water systems for per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) in drinking water. It currently includes approximately 98,000 samples from 28 states over the 2016-2024 period.
